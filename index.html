Thermal Fogging Technology

Thermal fogging is the generation of ultra-fine droplets in a range of 1-50 μm using thermo-pneumatic energy. Liquid substances are vaporized in the unit and form ultra-fine aerosols by condensing on contact with cool ambient air.
